Tournaments

1. Tournament Gameplay and Rules

 

a)    Each Tournament will have a start and an end time, which will be displayed at the point of entry.

b)    During this time, players can compete with their Blocklete golfers to try and get the highest score on the leaderboard. The highest score is the highest score in any one round.

c)    Players can play with a Blocklete golfer they own as many rounds as they would like during the duration of the Tournament time period.

d)    However, each round will have an “energy cost” that is known prior to playing. Each time a Blocklete golfer is used, energy is drained. The energy level of the golfer impacts the golfer’s abilities in the game. Energy rebuilds over a time period determined by that Blocklete golfer’s Stamina ability.

e)    If the Blocklete golfer’s energy level is below the energy cost of the Tournament, that golfer will be blocked from playing until the golfer’s energy restores back to the Tournament’s required level.

f)     In the event of a scoring tie, the Blocklete golfer that finished earlier wins the tiebreaker.

a.    For instance, two Blocklete golfers score 1000 points.

b.    Blocklete golfer A finished the round at 1:00pm

c.     Blocklete golfer B finished the found at 2:00 pm

d.    Blocklete golfer A will be ruled to finish ahead of Blocklete golfer B on the leaderboard.

 

2. How You Learn About a Tournament

 

Players can learn about live or upcoming Tournaments from the Blocklete Golf website Events page.  At the Events page, there will be a card that lists live and upcoming Tournaments and provides certain details about the Tournament. Please click the card to see Tournament details, such as:

●     Start and End Time

●     Difficulty

●     Wind: the range of wind to expect in the Tournament

●     Hazards: like Sand or Water

●     Prize table: This will show the prizes that will be awarded in the Tournament based on where you place.

○     The prizes will be in awarded in the ETH cryptocurrency (see https://ethereum.org/en/eth/ for more details about ETH)

●     Energy Cost: how much energy it will drain from your Blocklete golfer. This will vary by tournament.

●     Special eligibility requirements: Certain tournaments will have eligibility requirements in addition to the ones stated in these Official Rules, and these may be based on specific attributes of a Blocklete golfer (e.g., NFT token number, shirt color, hat/no-hat, etc.), or the Blocklete golfer’s past performance in Blocklete Golf (e.g., must have earned 100+ medals), or other criteria we specify.

 

3. How To Enter & Play in a Tournament

 

You can enter a Tournament by clicking “Play” on the Tournament details page. From here, you select which Blocklete golfer to play with from among the Blocklete golfers you own. You can play with any of your Blocklete golfers that meet the Tournament eligibility criteria.

 

The Golfer Details page (which is navigated to from My Clubhouse) may also list out upcoming Tournaments for which your Blocklete golfers are eligible.

 

4. Winner Payouts

 

After a Tournament concludes and the results are calculated and confirmed, prizes will be sent to the winning players’ digital wallets.

 

You can view your current balance in your Blocklete golf account from the My Clubhouse page and make withdrawals from there.
